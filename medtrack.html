Medtrack - Native app iOS and Android

Empowering Health Management with

This app empowers health management by not only helping users/patients solve problems with remembering to take their medication properly but also facilitating communication with doctors. The UI phase involved understanding native iOS and Android patterns for mobile devices. I used advanced prototyping to test with iOS and Android users.

Problem Statement

This app will helps users/patients solve their problems with forgetting taking their Medication properly on time and forgetting to call doctor for perscription or preventive inspection.

My Role

My responsibilities encompassed enhancing the overall user experience (UX) and user interface (UI) through activities such as crafting user flows, sketching wireframes both on paper and digitally, and conducting testing sessions with users.

Solution

I dedicated efforts to designing a user centric pill reminder app that simplifies medication management and fosters ongoing communication with healthcare providers. This app ensures users remain informed about their treatment plans and can easily connect with their doctors through messaging and calls..

Conclusion

In this UI project, I have explored what I needed and gained a clear idea of how the routing of the Medtrack app could develop. I have learned how important it is to think in detail about user needs to understand how they behave when they are using the app to remember their medication and how to make the app useful with advantageous features that are also understandable. I have thought about each screen down to the finest details.
